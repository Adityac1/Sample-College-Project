WHAT IS MIXED REALITY

It is the merging of real and virtual worlds to produce new Environments and visualizations, where physical and digital objects co-exist and interact in real time.

WHAT IS VIRTUAL REALITY

The computer-generated simulation of a three-dimensional image or environment that can be interacted with in a seemingly real or physical way be a person using special electronic equipment , such as a helmet with a screen.

WHAT IS AUGUMENTED REALITY

A technology that superimposes a computer-generated image on a user’s view of the real world , thus providing a composite view.

HOW IT WORKS!

The Hololens has a plethora of optical sensors, with two on each side for peripheral “environment understanding” sensing, a main downward facing depth camera to pick up hand motions, and specialized speakers that simulate sound from anywhere in the room.
